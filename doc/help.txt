
Wordefin - A Compact Studying Tool

Usage: wordefin [options]

Options:
	-h, --help	Outputs this page.

	-g			Launches Wordefin GUI.

	-t 			Launches Wordefin in current terminal.

Default (no options given) is to launch Wordefin in current terminal (same as -t flag.

Wordefin is a simple GUI/TUI studying tool, coded in perl and bash. It allows you to find word definitions based on .txt/.md/.lst word lists. Any desired word lists should be located in ~/Wordefin/ and in a similiar format to ~/Wordefin/example.txt.
